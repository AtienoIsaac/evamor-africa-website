# Climate-Smart Agriculture

Climate-smart agriculture is a key part of Evamor Africa's work to strengthen food systems while responding to climate change. We support agricultural practices that improve productivity, enhance resilience, and reduce environmental impact.

## Our Focus

Changing climate patterns continue to affect food security and rural livelihoods. Our programs promote adaptive farming methods that help communities cope with climate risks while protecting natural resources.

## What We Do

<ul class="content-list">
  <li>Promote sustainable and climate-resilient farming practices</li>
  <li>Support smallholder farmers through training and knowledge sharing</li>
  <li>Encourage soil and water conservation techniques</li>
  <li>Advocate for sustainable agricultural policies</li>
</ul>

## Why It Matters

Climate-smart agriculture supports food security, protects ecosystems, and strengthens community resilience in the face of climate change.
