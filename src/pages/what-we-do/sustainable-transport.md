# Sustainable Transport

Sustainable transport is essential for reducing emissions and improving access to services. Evamor Africa promotes transport solutions that are environmentally responsible, climate-resilient, and socially inclusive.

## Our Focus

Transport systems contribute significantly to greenhouse gas emissions and are often vulnerable to extreme weather. We focus on practical alternatives that lower emissions while still improving mobility for people and goods.

## What We Do

<ul class="content-list">
  <li>Promote low-emission mobility options, including electric delivery and cycling pathways</li>
  <li>Support climate-resilient transport infrastructure planning</li>
  <li>Explore transport routes that are affordable, safe, and accessible to communities</li>
  <li>Advocate for sustainable transport policies</li>
  <li>Raise awareness on transport-related environmental impacts</li>
</ul>

## Why It Matters

Sustainable transport improves mobility, reduces emissions, and strengthens resilience to climate-related disruptions while creating room for green jobs and enterprise.
